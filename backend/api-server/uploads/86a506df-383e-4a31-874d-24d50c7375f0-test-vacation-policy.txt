# Company Vacation Policy

## Overview
Our company believes in work-life balance and provides comprehensive vacation benefits to all employees.

## Vacation Entitlement
- New employees: 15 days per year
- Employees with 2+ years: 20 days per year  
- Employees with 5+ years: 25 days per year
- Senior staff (10+ years): 30 days per year

## How to Request Time Off
1. Submit vacation request through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Get approval from your direct manager
3. Ensure proper coverage for your responsibilities
4. Add vacation dates to the shared team calendar

## Vacation Policy Rules
- Maximum of 5 consecutive vacation days without special approval
- Vacation days cannot be carried over to the next year
- Emergency time off requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis
- All vacation requests must be approved before booking travel

## Contact Information
For questions about vacation policy, contact HR at hr@company.com or extension 1234.

## Holidays
Company observes all federal holidays plus 2 additional floating personal days.

## Sick Leave
Sick leave is separate from vacation time. Employees receive 10 sick days per year.
